## Original Wix Page Logic

The original `/metal` page is the cleanest of the craft pages: it focuses on one object and repeats it through angle, scale, and detail.

The old logic appears to be:

- Begin with a full-object hero image on a plain background, showing the circular/faceted metal form as a whole.
- Follow with alternate full views that reveal the object as a radial folded structure.
- Use side/profile images to show the sharp triangular rhythm and how the fins rise from the base.
- Use closeups to show fasteners, folded sheet-metal edges, reflections, and the inner cavity.
- Keep the page short and visual. The repeated views are the explanation.

For the rebuild, do not turn this into a long case study. It should be a precise object documentation page: title, one short sentence, then a carefully paced sequence of images.
