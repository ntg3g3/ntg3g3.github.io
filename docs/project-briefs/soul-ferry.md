## Original Wix Page Logic

The old page appears to be structured like an immersive experience pitch. It begins with atmospheric and concept images: boat, lotus, lanterns, character / spirit art, and dark river scenes. This establishes emotional tone before asking the viewer to understand mechanics.

The middle of the page shifts into interaction and level logic: rowing diagrams, route / node maps, and object models explain how the VR experience is controlled and staged. The final portion shows actual VR screenshots and the recovered motion loop.

The layout should preserve that emotional-to-system progression. Large images should carry atmosphere first. Diagrams should be grouped later as explanatory material. The motion loop should appear after the viewer understands what the boat journey is, not as an isolated media asset.
